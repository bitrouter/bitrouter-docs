## Welcome to BitRouter Rewards

BitRouter's rewards program gives you cashback and API credits every time you use our platform. Plus, you can earn even more by inviting friends and building your network.

**What you earn:**
- **Points** - API credits you can use for any model (1 point = $1)
- **USDC Cashback** - Real money you can withdraw
- **Referral Rewards** - Ongoing earnings from your network's activity

<Callout type="info">
This rewards program is subject to change as we refine the platform. We're committed to ensuring any changes remain fair to all users.
</Callout>

### Step 2: Start Using the API

Use your trial credit and start earning cashback:

```bash
# Every API call earns you rewards
curl -X POST https://api.aimo.network/v1/chat/completions \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "model": "deepseek/deepseek-v3.2",
    "messages": [{"role": "user", "content": "Hello!"}]
  }'
```

Every dollar you spend automatically earns:
- **15% back in points** (Bronze tier)
- **5% back in USDC** (Bronze tier)

## FAQ

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="How do referrals work?">
    **Levels:** L1 = people you directly refer. L2 = people your L1s refer (Silver+). L3 = people your L2s refer (Gold only). A referral is valid when they sign up through your link, verify email, and make a $1+ transaction. "Active" referrals (for Gold tier) must have spent $10+ and used the API in the last 30 days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do points work?">
    Points are auto-applied to your next API calls (1 point = $1). They don't expire during beta but cannot be transferred. When you have both points and USDC, points are applied first, then any remaining balance is charged to your USDC.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does USDC cashback work?">
    Minimum withdrawal is $5 USDC. Payouts happen automatically every Monday to your connected wallet (Solana or Base). Gas fees are free during beta.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the Gold tier requirements?">
    You need $500 personal spend, $2,000 network volume (total L1 + L2 spend, all-time), 10 active L1 referrals, and 30+ day account age. Inactive referrals still count toward your total but not toward the "10 active" requirement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I lose my tier?">
    Not during the beta phase. After beta, we may introduce tier decay for accounts inactive for 60+ days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my company get Gold tier?">
    Yes! Contact us at team@aimo.network to discuss partnership options for companies and DAOs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will the reward percentages change?">
    Possibly after beta ends. All beta users will be grandfathered at current rates for a minimum of 6 months.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the fair play rules?">
    We want to keep the program fair for everyone. **Not allowed:** self-referrals, fake transactions, multiple accounts from the same wallet, and wash trading. We use email & wallet verification, minimum $10 transactions for tier progress, and pattern detection. Violations result in warnings, tier revocation, or reward clawback depending on severity.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
