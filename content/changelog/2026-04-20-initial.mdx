## Added

- New **Resources** dropdown in the site header consolidating Blog, Changelog, Community, Careers, Brand, and Legal.
- **Changelog** page (this one) backed by MDX — every future release gets its own entry.
- **Community** page with channel cards, contributor marquee, and links to every social surface.
- **Brand** page mirroring the [`bitrouter/brand-kit`](https://github.com/bitrouter/brand-kit) repo.
- **Legal** index with dedicated Privacy and Terms pages.
- Telegram and Reddit joined the social rotation (GitHub · Discord · Telegram · Reddit · X).

## Changed

- Privacy and Terms moved out of `/docs/overview` into a dedicated `/legal` namespace. Old URLs redirect.
- Footer now appears site-wide with the full social cluster, theme toggle, and resource links.
