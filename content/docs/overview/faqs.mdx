  <Accordion title="How is BitRouter different from mainstream API unifier like OpenRouter?">
      BitRouter was inspired by OpenRouter but with a different focus:
      - **Permissionless**: BitRouter operates on a decentralized network with no KYC, Geo-restriction, or any other form of censorship.
      - **Stablecoins over fiat**: BitRouter offers more customization options for users.
      - **Agent-Native**: BitRouter fosters a strong community of developers and users.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm a model provider — how do I onboard to BitRouter?">
      We're not yet open for public provider onboarding, but we'd love to hear from you. If you're a provider interested in listing your models on BitRouter, please reach out to us at [contact@bitrouter.ai](mailto:contact@bitrouter.ai) or join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/MkeG9Zwuaw) and let us know about your service.

      In the meantime, if you want to run the proxy for your own use, check out our [Self-Host Options](/docs/overview/self-host) guide.
  </Accordion>
