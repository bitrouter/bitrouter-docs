## Build Smarter with BitRouter

Whether you're a startup shipping your first AI agent or an enterprise scaling thousands, BitRouter provides the advanced infrastructure, custom solutions, and hands-on support to help you move faster.

## Advanced Features

- **Priority support & dedicated onboarding** — hands-on guidance from our team to get you up and running
- **Custom routing rules & provider pinning** — tailor routing logic to your specific compliance and performance needs
- **Volume-based pricing** — competitive rates that scale with your usage
- **Team spending controls** — scoped [Payment Delegation Keys](/docs/overview/agent-native-control#payment-delegation-key-pdk) with per-agent budgets and expiration
- **Self-hostable infrastructure** — run the entire routing layer on your own servers with [bitrouter](https://github.com/bitrouter/bitrouter)
- **SLA guarantees & uptime commitments** — reliability you can build on
- **Custom model & fine-tuning integration** — bring your own models or fine-tunes into the routing layer
- **Advanced analytics & usage reporting** — deep visibility into spend, latency, and usage across teams and projects
