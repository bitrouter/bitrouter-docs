## Service Description

BitRouter is an AI proxy service that routes requests to various AI providers including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and others. We provide a unified interface for accessing multiple AI models with flexible payment options.

## Payment Terms

- **Credits**: Pre-paid balance for API usage
- **x402 Crypto Payments**: Pay-per-request using cryptocurrency overpayments

All payments are final. Unused credits may be refunded upon request. Crypto overpayments are automatically credited to your account balance.
